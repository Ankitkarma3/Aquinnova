About Aquinnova

Established in 2018, we are a leading pharmaceutical distributor based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh

Wholesaler & Distributor of Pharmaceuticals

Aquinnova Pharmaceuticals Private Limited is an Indian company established in March 2018, specializing in wholesale distribution of various pharmaceutical products including syrups, tablets, protein powders, vitamin supplements, and nutraceuticals.

Operating from Indore, Madhya Pradesh, we have built a reputation for quality and reliability in the Indian pharmaceutical supply chain. Our products are available through major online pharmacies and healthcare platforms across India.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable pharmaceutical products that contribute to improved health outcomes across India through efficient distribution and reliable supply chain management.
